iSimon - The Virtual Light and Sound Memory Game

Welcome to iSimon, a game that puts your recall and recognition skills to the test! Your objective is to repeat the pattern by clicking the corresponding buttons below.

Game Rules

The pattern is randomly generated. There are 6 rounds, at the beginning of each a portion of the pattern will be revealed. Repeat the pattern to advance to the next round. You have three strikes before the game ends. The reveal duration will decrease at higher rounds, so stay frosty!
